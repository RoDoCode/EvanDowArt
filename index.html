Evan Dow

"There is nothing higher than reason"

About Me

Born in Dublin, Evan spent time growing in London. The son of two successful actors he changed address many times during his formative years, following their careers between the UK and the Republic of Ireland. After 5 years in Belfast at the age of 21, Evan migrated back to the UK to study fine art at Newcastle University. He then undertook 4 years of fruitful study in the UK before returning finally to Dublin, his birthplace, to take his Masters in Fine Art Painting. His time studying allowed him to hone a range of media:

  • Paint in oils/watercolours/acrylics
  • Sculpture in clay/metals/stone
  • Thought for thoughts sake
  • Ink Work
  • Pencil sketching
  • Mixed media works

Evan has spent the last 3 years focusing on the past works of great minds who came before to understand their teachings on the nature of being. The being of the ordinary. The world we take for granted which holds a deeper meaning. The nature of commonplace inanimate objects and what they actually are. His works are studies on the extraordinary status ordinary things can hold when looked at through the artists lense.

Services

Art Research Projects

With 6 years of formal artistic academic training Evan is open to private research projects. Focusing on philosophical topics or personal stories. Get in touch if you have a study in mind so we can frame the scope of the project and understand what might be undertaken.

Painting Commission

Evan's commissions have already been recommended by a patron in the Irish Radio Times. His works take a range of different form factors. If you have a personal favourite let him know while arrange your commission. Or show him the space you intend to display in and simply tell him your story.

Sculpture Commission

Breathing life into a sculpture is a labour of love. Evan has nurtured a range of sculture into being. If you have something in mind or are interested in owning his next piece based on his private studies and journey; get in touch.

Collection Tour

Evan's existing back collection is extensive. If you are seriously interested in his works he is open to private visits to his studio space in the centre of Dublin.

Exhibitions

Several galleries have already exhibited some of Evan's early works. If you are interested in hosting an exhibition Evan would be pleased to hear from you and discuss what could be arranged based on the size of the space and notice period. Private "at-home" exhibition parties are also open for discussion.

QA

Evan is passionate about his work and the theory around it. Whether you are a collector, a fellow artist or a young person interested in getting started, he is always open to questions. The contact form is near the bottom of this page and we encourage you to reach out with your queries.
